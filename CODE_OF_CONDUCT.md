
# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct
## Our Pledge
We, the contributors and maintainers of the **Real-Time Markdown Editor**, pledge to make participation in this project a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, mental health, ethnicity, nationality, or religion.

## Our Standards
- **Be respectful** – Treat others with courtesy and professionalism.
- **Use inclusive language** – Avoid slurs, jokes, or remarks that target protected groups.
- **Stay on topic** – Keep discussions focused on the project’s goals and technical content.
- **No harassment** – This includes intimidation, stalking, unwanted sexual attention, or any form of discrimination.
- **Respect privacy** – Do not share personal data of others without explicit permission.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers at **contact@sumanjangili.com** (or via the GitHub “Report abuse” button). All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly. Maintainers reserve the right to:
- Warn the offending individual,
- Temporarily ban the individual from the repository,
- Permanently remove the individual’s access.
